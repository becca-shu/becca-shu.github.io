How does it work?

In-person readings are available in New York City. Once you contact me to request a reading, we will agree on a mutually convenient location, and a meeting time that works for both of us.

If you request an email reading, the reading will be emailed to you within a week, after I confirm that I’ve received your question.

Cash, check, PayPal, and Venmo are all acceptable forms of payment. For in-person readings, payment will be due at the end of the reading. For email readings, payment will be due upon receipt of the reading.

Referrals

Refer a friend to me – if they book a reading, you’ll get a 25% discount on your next reading!
