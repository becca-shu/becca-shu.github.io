How does it work?

In-person readings are available in New York City. Once you contact me to request a reading, we will agree on a mutually convenient location, and a meeting time that works for both of us.

If you're not able to meet in New York City for an in-person reading with me, you can request a written email reading through the contact form. Once we’ve settled on your question for the cards and I’ve received your payment, I’ll email you the reading within five business days. The written email reading will be a document explaining the cards I’ve pulled to answer your question, a summary of takeaways from the spread, and pictures of the cards.

Cash, check, PayPal, and Venmo are all acceptable forms of payment.
